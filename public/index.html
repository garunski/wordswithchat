Words With Chat

A tiny game of guessing words.

Get ready to challenge your vocabulary in a thrilling game of word guesses with ChatGPT! Start by selecting your favorite category: Animals, Places, or Colors. Your choice sets the stage for the game. Once you've made your selection, ChatGPT dives into its extensive word library, using a bank of words to fetch a random word from your chosen category. The fun begins as you try to guess this word based on clever hints provided by ChatGPT. Remember, the more accurate your guesses, the closer you get to the word!

But that's not all! The game is a balance of wit and strategy. ChatGPT will offer you hints, but be cautious - these hints are helpful yet subtle, designed to guide you without giving away the word. Your task is to interpret these hints and make your guess. Correct guesses are rewarded. And if curiosity about your progress gets the better of you, just ask! ChatGPT can retrieve your current score anytime with a simple request.

Are you ready to play?

Let the guessing game begin!
